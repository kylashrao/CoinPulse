About CoinPulse

Most crypto price sites use the word "live" without explaining what that actually means. CoinPulse was built around a simpler idea: tell people exactly how fresh the data is, and let them decide if that's good enough for what they're doing.

We pull price data from CoinGecko's free public API, which is typically cached for one to five minutes on its end. We check for updates every 60 seconds and always display exactly when the price was last updated — not just when your browser last made a request.

🎯

Transparent timing

We show the real "last updated" timestamp from the data source, not a fake live indicator.

🌍

Multi-currency

Prices in USD, GBP, CAD, and AUD, switchable instantly without reloading the page.

🆓

No account, no paywall, no limit on how many times you check a price.

Where the data comes from

All price data on CoinPulse is sourced from CoinGecko's public market data API, which aggregates pricing across a wide range of cryptocurrency exchanges. We don't operate our own price feed, hold any cryptocurrency, or have any trading relationship with the coins listed.

Not financial advice: CoinPulse exists to display price information clearly, not to recommend buying, selling, or holding any cryptocurrency. Prices are highly volatile. Please do your own research before making any financial decisions.
