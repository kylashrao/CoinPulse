Privacy Policy

1. Overview

CoinPulse ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website coinpulse.app (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the Service, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

2. How price data is fetched

This Service does not operate its own backend server for price data. Instead, your browser makes a direct request to CoinGecko's public API each time prices are refreshed. This means CoinGecko may log standard request information (such as your IP address) as part of operating their API, governed by CoinGecko's own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review separately. We do not control or have access to these logs.

3. Information we collect

We may automatically collect limited technical information through standard web server logs and analytics for this Service, including browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location derived from IP address, and referring website.

If you use our contact form, we collect the name, email address, and message you choose to provide, solely to respond to your inquiry.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

This Service and its third-party partners may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Service, remember preferences, and serve relevant advertising. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of the Service may not function properly without them.

5. Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this Service. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at www.aboutads.info.

6. Third-party data and links

Cryptocurrency price data displayed on this Service is provided by CoinGecko's public API. We are not affiliated with CoinGecko and do not control the accuracy, availability, or timing of their data. The Service may also contain links to third-party websites that are not owned or controlled by us; we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

7. Financial disclaimer

Content on this Service is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, investment, or trading advice. Cryptocurrency prices are highly volatile. You should conduct your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.

8. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at kylash.rao@gmail.com or via our contact page.
